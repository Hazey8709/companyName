    Questions / Answers

  1. How to contact us?

    2. You can use the nav-bar & click on Contact then use the provided Form or click Email US in the Quick-links section at bottom of page.

  2. Is estimate free?

    3. Yes of course.

  3. Estimate's?

    4. Estimate's are based on labor only(unless specified with materials) - (An estimate is a quote for labor to the best of our knowledge ACTUAL Price: May Vary.)

  4. Down Payments?

    5. Normal payment is 30% down to schedule appointment & 20% on day Job Starts & 50% on day finished. Other arrangements vary & can be discussed. Down Payments are to help cover material costs and will be deducted from total amount of bill. Receipt will be provided to customer and for record.

  5. Job Start/End

    6. Once a job is started it will be finished before starting another job.(One job at a time helps quality.) - (Unless theres an emergency)

  6. Billing

    7. Job will be itemized and saved To customers name for record keeping. No information will be shared for any reason, besides with customer or by customers consent. Receipts are giving for all transactions no exception!

  7. Job Alterations

    8. All alterations to the plans after agreement will be noted and billed accordingly. Alterations will require a sign off to be approved before changes are made. Alterations will be added to the bill and itemized list as Job Alteration.

  8. Job Stop Due To Payment Issues

    9. If a payment is missed without notice or communication Job will halt normal operation until rectified. Job then will be rescheduled if needed or presume normal operation.

  9. Documentation

    10. Paperwork & document fees. All customers will be charged a Doc Fee! Doc Fee covers paperwork, documentation costs and Secretary. Doc Fee is 25$ and will be applied to the bill.

  10. Information Policy

    11. No information will be shared for any reason, besides with customer or by customers consent. Receipts are giving for all transactions no exception! Any information collected will be used for no other purpose other than the job. Any price or discount will not be disclosed to any one other than customer.

  11. Warranty

    12. Warranty is offered by company only! Warranty will be agreed upon and signed off on. On the agreement it will show what is covered depending on warranty choice. Keep in mind warranties are limited and only cover certain issues. Labor is not included unless specified!
